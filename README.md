CorpConnect is a web app built to make it easier for companies to manage employees, assign projects, and track work—all in one place.
In most organizations, managing people across projects is still chaotic—spreadsheets, emails, and constant back-and-forth. It’s hard to know who is available, who has the right skills, and how work is progressing.
CorpConnect brings everything together into a single, simple platform.
Create and update employee profiles
Assign the right people to the right projects
Get smart suggestions based on skills
Log daily work hours easily
Keep track of progress in real time
Get notified about updates
